# Deploying Starlight Checklists to GitHub Pages Your live URL will be: https://starlight-manager.github.io/Starlight-Checklists ## Steps 1. Go to github.com and log into Starlight-Manager account 2. Open the Starlight-Checklists repository 3. Click "Add file" → "Upload files" 4. Drag ALL of these files/folders in: - index.html - css/ (the whole folder) - js/ (the whole folder) 5. Scroll down, click "Commit changes" 6. Go to Settings → Pages 7. Under Source, select: Deploy from a branch → main → / (root) 8. Click Save 9. Wait 2-3 minutes, then visit your URL ## Staff Access Just share this link: https://starlight-manager.github.io/Starlight-Checklists They bookmark it on their phone. No login needed.
